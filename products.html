Why Electrolytic?

Electrolytic vs. Atomized Copper Powder

PropertyElectrolytic Copper (Our Product)Atomized Copper
Production MethodElectrochemical depositionMolten metal atomization
MorphologyDendritic (tree-like branching)Spherical to irregular
Apparent Density Range0.6–4.5 g/cm³ (wide range)2.5–5.5 g/cm³
Specific Surface AreaHigh (due to dendrites)Lower (smoother surfaces)
Green StrengthExcellentGood
Purity>99.80% (our standard)>99.0% typical
CompressibilityExcellentGood
Sintering BehaviorFast (high surface area)Moderate
Best ForFriction, PM, Diamond Tools, Catalysts3D printing, Thermal spray, Filters
